Great food, innovative Lassi servings, beautiful setting

This is one 24 Hour restaurant with an imposing sight that cannot be missed even if you want to. The Restaurant can be seen a mile away from the Chandigarh, Amritsar Highway. This is truly a pure North Indian Punjabi vegetarian Dhabha. Everything is served fresh and hot and the food is top notch quality both in terms of portions and taste. The Lassi was served in a earthen pot and was very very tasty- maybe because of the container it was served in. The setting even before entering the restaurant is nice and inviting.
